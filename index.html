About Me

A good photograph is a treasure. It freezes a memory forever, there to remind us of great moments, of time spent together, and when our children were a few inches shorter. Although pictures are a simple thing, they are often some of our most prized possessions.

I've been a photographer since early 2016, when I recognized how important it was to me to have these memories of my young children before they grew up. While I started with family photos, I've done a wide variety of photography; senior portraits, weddings, engagements, babies, and team photos. With all of these experiences, my expertise and my favorite shots are still family photos; they always remind me of why photography is important to me.

I prefer light and airy sessions; bright outdoor locations are my favorite, where I can take advantage of the beautiful vistas throughout Utah and Nevada. When I don't have photoshoots, I'm a substitute elementary school teacher, and I'm learning how to code to be a front-end developer. I am a tech-mom. I have four beautiful children and a supportive, hard-working husband. In my fleeting spare time, I enjoy traveling to warm places with beaches and tacos, reading, and playing volleyball.
